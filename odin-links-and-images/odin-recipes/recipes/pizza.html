Prep Time

1 hour 15 minutes

Cook Time

20 minutes

Servings

4 people

Italian Appetizer Dinner Main Course

Ingredients

Dough

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110째F/43째C)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Sauce

  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Toppings

  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil

Instructions

  1. 1

    In a large bowl, combine flour, yeast, salt, and sugar.

  2. 2

    Add warm water and olive oil, stir until dough forms.

  3. 3

    Knead dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes.

  4. 4

    Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.

  5. 5

    Preheat oven to 475째F (245째C).

  6. 6

    Roll out dough into a 12-inch circle.

  7. 7

    Spread sauce evenly, add toppings.

  8. 8

    Bake for 15-20 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

  9. 9

    Let cool for 5 minutes before slicing.

Cooking Tips

For a crispy crust, preheat your oven and baking stone (if using) for at least 30 minutes before baking.
