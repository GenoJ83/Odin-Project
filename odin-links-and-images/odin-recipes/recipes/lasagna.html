Classic Lasagna

Home Recipes About Contact

Prep Time

30 minutes

Cook Time

45 minutes

Servings

6 people

Italian Pasta Main Course Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. 1

    In a Dutch oven, cook beef, onion, and garlic over medium-high heat until beef is browned. Drain grease.

  2. 2

    Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 1 hour.

  3. 3

    Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  4. 4

    To prepare the ricotta mixture, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining salt and pepper, and 2 tablespoons Parmesan.

  5. 5

    Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

  6. 6

    Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.

  7. 7

    Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes.

  8. 8

    Cool for 15 minutes before serving.

Cooking Tips

For the best results, use fresh ingredients and let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving.
